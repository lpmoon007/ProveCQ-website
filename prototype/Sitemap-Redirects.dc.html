Technical SEO/GEO checklist

  • sitemap.xml + robots.txt; submit to Google Search Console & Bing.
  • JSON-LD: Organization, WebSite, FAQPage (on guides w/ Q&A), Article (Metric + guides), BreadcrumbList.
  • Unique <title> + meta description per page (already drafted in each prototype <helmet>).
  • Per-page OG/Twitter cards; canonical tags; 301 (not 302) for all FPO redirects to pass equity.
  • GEO: keep the "short answer" boxes, FAQ blocks, comparison tables, and definitional H2s — they're what LLM answer engines extract and cite.
  • commitment-quotient.com: 301 into provecq.com (or canonical to it) to avoid duplicate content.
