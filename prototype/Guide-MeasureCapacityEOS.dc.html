How to measure capacity in EOS — beyond the checkbox.

GWC is the heart of the People Analyzer, but Capacity gets scored as a gut-feel yes or no. That blind spot is where great hires stall and Rocks slip. Here is how to make it measurable.

The short answer

Capacity is not one thing and it is not static — it is the behavioral ability to deliver a specific role right now. Measure it as Initiative, Applied Grit, and Learnability from observed behavior, and the Capacity box becomes real data you can bring to Quarterly Conversations.

Why the checkbox fails

01

Capacity is dynamic

It shifts with life stage, bandwidth, and unseen stress. A one-time yes/no cannot capture that.

02

It gets confused with GW

Leaders mark Capacity down when they really mean the person does not Get it or Want it — muddying the whole analyzer.

What to measure instead

Initiative

Acts on the seat without being pushed.

Applied Grit

Delivers the hard parts of the role, not just the easy ones.

Learnability

Grows into rising demands as the company scales.
